About

Hi! I'm Alyssa. As a recovering perfectionist, I work on the principle that improvement is always possible, but not always necessary. With that in mind, I aim to do everything to an exceptional level, without being crippled by the illusive and unattainable 'perfect', that often prevents great work from being achieved.

Although I'm relatively new to web development, I feel I've finally found the career that I was meant to do. In no other profession have I found the perfect mix of logic and specificity, mixed with creativity in both problem-solving and design, that is inherent to web development.
